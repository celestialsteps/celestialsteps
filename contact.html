Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need for a reading?

For the most accurate reading, I need your exact date, time, and place of birth. If you don't know your birth time, we can still do a reading, though it will be less detailed.

How long are the readings?

Birth chart readings typically take 60-90 minutes, while focused consultations are usually 30-45 minutes. I always ensure you have time for all your questions.

Do you offer remote readings?

Yes! I conduct readings via video call, phone, or detailed written reports. Distance doesn't affect the accuracy of astrological insights.

How far in advance should I book?

I recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance, though I sometimes have last-minute availability. Use the booking link above to see current openings.
